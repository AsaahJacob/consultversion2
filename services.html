Our Services

Explore the diverse services offered by Rasheena Reid, combining legal expertise, education, immigration, and development consulting.

Consultancy Services

  • Tax Law Advisory: U.S. and international tax law guidance for compliance, planning, and regulatory matters.
  • Foreign Policy & Diplomatic Strategy: Consultation for agencies navigating international relations and diplomatic protocol.
  • International Development: Advisory services for NGOs/governments on development programs and humanitarian initiatives.
  • Immigration Policy: Organizational and individual consulting on immigration regulations and compliance.
  • Multilingual Program Development: Training programs in Spanish and Portuguese for development professionals and diplomats.

Legal Services

  • Tax Law Counsel: Legal advice on taxation, especially for expatriates doing business in the U.S.
  • Immigration Assistance: Visa applications, residency guidance, and legal support for professionals on assignment.
  • Legal Research & Policy Analysis: In-depth research and drafting policy recommendations on legal issues.

Teaching & Tutoring Services

  • Language Instruction: Spanish and Portuguese lessons for all levels with a focus on real-world fluency and cultural understanding.
  • Mathematics Tutoring: Math tutoring for high school and adult learners tailored to individual learning styles.
  • Curriculum Design: Custom lesson plans for schools, adult learners, or corporate education initiatives.

Additional Services

  • Cross-Disciplinary Training: Workshops blending legal, immigration, and language skills for international professionals.
  • Mentoring & Coaching: Academic and career mentoring in law, diplomacy, immigration, and global careers.
