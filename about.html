About Rasheena Reid

Rasheena Reid is a uniquely qualified professional whose career spans law, diplomacy, education, and international development. A tax attorney by training, a former U.S. diplomat, and an experienced educator, Rasheena brings a multidisciplinary and global perspective to her consultancy services.

Over the course of a distinguished 15-year diplomatic career, Rasheena served in U.S. Embassies around the world, where she advised senior U.S. and foreign government officials on complex foreign policy matters and spearheaded development initiatives aimed at fostering sustainable growth in emerging countries. Her work with the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) took her to Peru, Afghanistan, the Dominican Republic, Liberia, and Ghana—where she designed and managed programs in economic development, education, governance, and humanitarian aid.

Rasheena’s academic credentials are equally impressive. She holds a Bachelor of Arts from Yale University, a Juris Doctor and Master’s in Education from the University of Pennsylvania, and an LL.M. in Taxation from Georgetown Law. This robust educational foundation equips her with a deep understanding of U.S. and international tax law, educational policy, and legal frameworks affecting global development.

In addition to her legal and diplomatic accomplishments, Rasheena is a dedicated educator. She began her career as a Teach For America corps member, teaching high school Spanish, and has continued to share her knowledge of Spanish, Portuguese, and mathematics in both classroom and tutoring settings. Fluent in both Spanish and Portuguese, she has taught and worked extensively in Brazil, combining her linguistic skills with legal and educational expertise.

Rasheena Reid’s rare blend of legal acumen, diplomatic experience, and teaching excellence makes her an exceptional consultant in tax law, international development, humanitarian work, and multilingual education.

Specialized counsel in U.S. tax law and immigration processes for expatriates and global professionals.
